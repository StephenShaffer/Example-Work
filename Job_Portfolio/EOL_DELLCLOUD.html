Dear Symantec Customer,

This FAQ addresses commonly asked questions regarding Symantec’s decision to End of Life (EOL) Symantec Email Continuity.cloud and Email Archiving.cloud by Dell.
 
What is happening to Symantec Email Continuity.cloud and Email Archiving.cloud by Dell? On February 2, 2015, Symantec will cease selling Symantec Email Continuity.cloud by Dell and Email Archiving.cloud by Dell. As a result, we are working with Dell to provide you with an easy migration. If you are an existing customer, you may continue to use Symantec Email Continuity.cloud by Dell and Email Archiving.cloud by Dell until the end of service availability on February 1, 2016, or the end of your current service agreement, whichever is sooner.

After February 1, 2016, or the end of your current service agreement, whichever is sooner, you will no longer be able to log in to Symantec Email Continuity.cloud by Dell and Email Archiving.cloud by Dell service and technical support for these services will no longer be available. If you have transitioned to Dell before this date you will not be impacted.

Please note that this announcement impacts only Email Continuity.cloud by Dell and Email Archiving.cloud by Dell. Other Symantec archiving products, such as Symantec Email Security.cloud, Symantec Enterprise Vault Email Continuity.cloud, Symantec Enterprise Vault. cloud and Enterprise Vault are NOT impacted.
Why was the decision made? Our goal is to delight customers with our product and services offerings. Taking this into consideration and carefully evaluating the overall needs of our customer base, we will continue to focus efforts on our archiving portfolio including Enterprise Vault and Enterprise Vault.cloud.
What is Symantec's strategic plan for cloud archiving and other cloud products? Discontinuing Email Continuity.cloud by Dell and Email Archiving.cloud by Dell allows us sharpen our focus on Enterprise Vault.cloud. Product roadmaps and resources will continue to be targeted toward market leading features and functionality.

Symantec will continue to invest in cloud offerings that enable our customers to scale and protect their information as their needs grow. These offerings include Symantec Enterprise Vault.cloud, Symantec Enterprise Vault Email Continuity.cloud, Symantec Email Security.cloud, and Symantec Endpoint Protection Small Business Edition 2013.
What is the timeline for the EOS and EOA?
  • February 2, 2015: Subscription
    You will no longer be able to purchase a subscription (either new or renewal beyond the end date of your current service agreement).
  • February 1, 2016: Service and Technical Support
    You will no longer be able to access or use the Email Continuity.cloud & Email Archiving.cloud by Dell services and Technical Support will no longer be available, unless you have contracted directly with Dell.

We want to do everything we can to minimize any disruption to your businesses.

To this end, the timing of the Symantec Email Continuity.cloud by Dell and Email Archiving.cloud by Dell service discontinuation is designed to allow you ample time to contract for these services directly with Dell, or to another option of your choice. To retain these services beyond the end date of your current service agreement, you must enter into a separate services contract with Dell.

How do I get my data from the Email Archiving.cloud by Dell service if I do not choose to retain that service?
  • Self-Service Export - Export your data and terminate your existing service.
    If you wish to transition your existing data to another archiving service, such as Symantec Enterprise Vault.cloud, you will need to begin the self-service export process in advance of your service termination date as all data must be exported by that time. Data that is not exported at the service termination data will be deleted. If you need information about conducting a self-service export, please contact Dell Technical Support.
  • Assisted Export – Symantec/Dell will export your data for a per TB fee and deliver the data on secure media. Such extraction will need to be completed in advance of your service termination date as all data must be exported by that time. Symantec has procedures in place for data extraction out of the EmailArchive.cloud by Dell service.
  • No export - Terminate your service without exporting data. You may simply elect to allow your service agreement to lapse without exporting data. In which case and at that time you will no longer be able to log into the web portal, and your archived email data will be deleted.
Will I receive any kind of refund? Yes, if you have made a prepayment for a service period extending beyond the End of Service Life date, i.e. prepayment for service period extending beyond February 1, 2016.
I'm eligible for a refund, how does the process work? You are eligible for a refund if your contract’s then current term goes beyond the service’s End of Service Life on February 1, 2016. If you meet this eligibility requirement, please request the refund through your original point of purchase.
  • If you purchased through a reseller or partner, please contact your reseller or partner for a refund.
If you purchased directly from Symantec, Symantec will issue a refund directly to you. Please send an email that includes your order number to CLD_cancellations_MLABS@symantec.com.
What are my options to migrate to another Symantec archiving solution? Symantec's Enterprise Vault.cloud, an alternative to the Email Archiving.cloud by Dell offering, is a cloud-based archiving service that helps organizations store, manage, and discover business-critical information. Enterprise Vault.cloud securely captures information in a centralized and tamper-proof online repository, with seamless end user access and rapid search functionality. Plus, the built-in collaborative eDiscovery workflow provides legal teams with roles-based access, expediting the discovery process. With no mandatory hardware or software required, the service is quick and easy to deploy and provides unlimited storage for a predictable monthly fee.

Please contact your Symantec Account Representative for more information about this option. Note that you remain contractually obligated for payment of your current Email Continuity.cloud and/or Email Archiving.cloud by Dell as provided by your Symantec services agreement and the End of Availability/End of Service Announcement/Notice of Non-Renewal.
Will Symantec offer data migration services? Data migration will not be required if you elect to contract directly with Dell to retain the Email Archiving.cloud by Dell. Your services will continue without disruption. However, if you plan on migrating to Symantec Enterprise Vault.cloud, or a third party archiving vendor, please contact Symantec Technical Support. Symantec has a process defined for retrieving your data from Dell. The contact information for Technical Support is listed in this document.
Will there be any downtime with the migration? If you elect to contract directly with Dell to retain the Email Continuity.cloud and/or Email Archiving.cloud by Dell, there is no expected downtime.
