Re: HPE Software Spin-Merger with Micro Focus
 
Dear Customer:
 
On September 7, 2016, Hewlett Packard Enterprise Company (“HPE”) announced plans to spin-off and merge its HPE Software business unit (“SW Business”) with Micro Focus International plc (“Micro Focus”), a global software company dedicated to delivering and supporting enterprise software solutions (“SW Spin-Merger”). HPE’s decision will create two businesses that are stronger, more focused and better able to innovate and adapt in today’s market, delivering faster outcomes to our customers. The combination of the HPE SW Business with Micro Focus will create one of the world’s largest pure-play enterprise software companies.
 
Until the SW Spin-Merger is complete, which is currently targeted for September 1, 2017, HPE, including our SW Business, will remain a single organization and continue to operate under our agreements with you and your affiliates. This letter contains confidential information about the SW Spin-Merger that we are sharing with you under the confidentiality provisions of our existing agreements with you.
 
Your Agreements with HPE
 
HPE is reorganizing its business internally in preparation for the SW Spin-Merger by separating its SW Business from HPE’s other business assets and liabilities (“Remaining HPE Business”) among HPE subsidiaries. We would like to notify you that all of your agreements with HPE (and its subsidiaries, including Hewlett-Packard (Schweiz) GmbH and all associated agreements and documents (including, but not limited to, software license agreements, non-disclosure agreements, local service agreements, data transfer agreements, equipment loan agreements, and transaction documents) (the “Agreements”) will be separated or duplicated, and allocated among HPE subsidiaries as follows effective as of June 1, 2017 (the “Effective Date”).
 
1) Your Agreements (and all rights, duties and obligations under them) that relate exclusively to the HPE SW Business will be allocated to EntCo Schweiz GmbH, which is anticipated to merge with Micro Focus (and its subsidiaries) following completion of the SW Spin-Merger.
     
2) Your Agreements (and all rights, duties and obligations under them) that relate exclusively to the Remaining HPE Business will remain with HPE, which in Switzerland will be allocated to Hewlett-Packard (Schweiz) GmbH.
     
3) Your Agreements (and all rights, duties and obligations under them) that relate to both the HPE SW Business and the Remaining HPE Business will be divided, duplicated and assigned so that in effect, from the Effective Date, the rights and obligations referable to the HPE SW Business are under a separate agreement between you and EntCo Schweiz GmbH, and the rights and obligations referable to the Remaining HPE Business are under a separate agreement between you and Hewlett-Packard (Schweiz) GmbH, allowing you to transact business with each company for their respective offerings, without interruption.
     
Additional Information
 
All open orders with your current HPE contracting party will be transferred together with their related agreements on the Effective Date. We will communicate with you again soon with all required information for those open orders, as well as all other operational and logistical details, including legal entity names, addresses, and like information for HPE and the appropriate subsidiaries.
 
Upon completion of the SW Spin-Merger, EntCo Schweiz GmbH will merge with Micro Focus (and its subsidiaries).
 
Please communicate these changes throughout your organization, including affiliates, as appropriate.
 
Thank you in advance for your prompt attention to this matter.
